History

The history of TechCon begins in 2012, when a group of tech enthusiasts who coincidentally lost their jobs decided to come together and provide a forum where people with interests in technology can meet and network. What started as a gathering with 15 people quickly grew, and now TechCon is one of the most anticipated conferences in the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation among passionate tech enthusiasts. We aim to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, networking, and showing the latest trends in technology.
